Our Story

From Community Need to Community Care

Medhome Medicals was established in Marangattupilly with a simple mission — to make quality healthcare products accessible and affordable to every household in the region.

Recognizing the need for a reliable pharmacy the community could trust, we set up our store at Kattikkanal Building, near Labour India Complex. Since then, we've served thousands of families with genuine medicines, expert guidance, and unbeatable discounts.

What started as a neighbourhood pharmacy has grown into a trusted healthcare destination, known for quality, affordability, and free home delivery.
